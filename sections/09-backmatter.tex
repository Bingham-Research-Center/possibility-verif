\section*{Notation Reference}\label{sec:notation_reference}

Table~\ref{tab:full_notation} collects all recurring symbols, their
\LaTeX{} macros, mathematical meanings, and which form of the
possibility distribution (raw $\pi$ or normalised $\pinorm$) each uses.

\begin{table}[htbp]
\centering
\caption{Complete notation reference for possibilistic verification.}
\label{tab:full_notation}
\small
\begin{tabularx}{\columnwidth}{llclX}
\toprule
Symbol & Macro & Form & Eq. & Meaning \\
\midrule
$\Omega$
    & ---
    & ---
    & ---
    & Universe of discourse (set of all outcomes) \\
$\pi$
    & ---
    & raw
    & ---
    & Raw (possibly subnormal) possibility distribution \\
$\pinorm$
    & \verb|\pinorm|
    & norm
    & ---
    & Normalised distribution: $\pinorm = \pi / m$ \\
$m$
    & ---
    & raw
    & ---
    & Subnormality: $m = \max_{\omega} \pi(\omega)$ \\
$\poss(A)$
    & \verb|\poss|
    & raw
    & \ref{eq:poss}
    & Possibility of event $A$: $\max_{\omega \in A} \pi(\omega)$ \\
$\nec(A)$
    & \verb|\nec|
    & raw
    & \ref{eq:nec}
    & Necessity of event $A$: $1 - \poss(\neg A)$ \\
$\Nc(A)$
    & \verb|\Nc|
    & norm
    & \ref{eq:cond_nec}
    & Conditional necessity: certainty given coverage \\
$\ign$
    & \verb|\ign|
    & raw
    & \ref{eq:ignorance}
    & Possibilistic ignorance: $1 - m$ \\
$\alpha^*$
    & ---
    & norm
    & \ref{eq:alpha_star}
    & Depth-of-truth: $\pinorm(c_{\mathrm{obs}})$ \\
$\eta$
    & ---
    & norm

    & Nonspecificity: $(1/K)\sum_c \pinorm(c)$ \\
$\delta$
    & ---
    & norm
    & \ref{eq:rg}
    & Resolution gap: $\alpha^* - \eta$ \\
$\Nc^*$
    & ---
    & norm
    & \ref{eq:nc_star}
    & Conditional necessity of truth: $\Nc(c_{\mathrm{obs}})$ \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabularx}
\end{table}
