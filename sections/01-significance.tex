\section*{Significance Statement}
\begingroup
\hyphenpenalty=10000
\exhyphenpenalty=10000
Possibilistic forecasts encode richer uncertainty than probabilities---they
distinguish ``this outcome is merely plausible'' from ``this outcome is
essentially certain'' while explicitly flagging when the forecasting system
lacks knowledge.  However, no systematic verification framework has been proposed for
evaluating such forecasts.  This paper proposes a framework to address this gap by providing a
five-number scorecard, a possibility-to-probability bridge to
probabilistic scoring, and a categorical verification lane based on
mode-derived threshold scores, together enabling rigorous assessment of
possibilistic forecast quality across three complementary verification
lanes.
\endgroup
