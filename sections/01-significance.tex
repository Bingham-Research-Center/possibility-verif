\section*{Significance Statement}
\begingroup
\hyphenpenalty=10000
\exhyphenpenalty=10000
Possibilistic forecasts encode richer uncertainty than probabilities---they
distinguish ``this outcome is merely plausible'' from ``this outcome is
essentially certain'' while explicitly flagging when the forecasting system
lacks knowledge.  However, no verification framework currently exists for
evaluating such forecasts.  This paper fills that gap by providing a
five-number scorecard, a possibility-to-probability bridge to probabilistic scoring, and an
interval log score, together enabling rigorous assessment of possibilistic
forecast quality across three complementary verification lanes.
\endgroup
